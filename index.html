01 · What this is

A walking-distance
treasure hunt.

01

Sellers open up.

Neighbors sign up to host a sale from their garage, yard, or stoop. One Saturday, all at once. The more neighbors, the bigger the haul.

02

Shoppers wander.

A free, shareable digital map shows every participating address. Park once, walk the neighborhood, find vintage furniture, vinyl, baby gear, plants, kitchen stuff, and the weird thing you didn't know you needed.

03

Walk the loop.

No tickets, no schedule, no order. Pull up the map on your phone, wander block to block, take breaks where you like, and head home when your arms are full.

02 · The area

A pocket of south-west Avondale.
Small enough to walk.

We redrew the boundary around where neighbors actually signed up. It now runs a full mile of Belmont, from Pulaski east to Kedzie, and south to Diversey. That is about double the area we started with. The shaded area grows as nearby neighbors sign up. Anyone in Avondale can register.
