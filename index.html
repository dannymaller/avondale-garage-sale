01 · What this is

A neighborhood-wide
treasure hunt.

01

Sellers open up.

Households across Avondale sign up to host a sale from their garage, yard, or stoop. One Saturday, all at once. The more neighbors, the bigger the haul.

02

Shoppers wander.

A free, shareable digital map shows every participating address. Park once, walk the neighborhood, find vintage furniture, vinyl, baby gear, plants, kitchen stuff, and the weird thing you didn't know you needed.

03

Walk the loop.

No tickets, no schedule, no order. Pull up the map on your phone, wander block to block, take breaks where you like, and head home when your arms are full.

02 · The area

A pocket of south-west Avondale.
Small enough to walk.

Our year-one target: about 30 walkable blocks between Belmont, Diversey, Pulaski, and Milwaukee. Live elsewhere in Avondale? Sign up anyway. It helps us figure out where to grow.
